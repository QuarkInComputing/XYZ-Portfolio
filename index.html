Who Am I

Hi, I'm Jack - but I'm sure you might have guessed that already.

I'm a 19 year old BSc Computer Science student from UHI Inverness. I have previously been awarded my HNC and HND at UHI Inverness in Computer Science both with an A grade. During my time at UHI Inverness I have cultivated a particular expertise in web & software development, being able to merge my existing design and programming skills into one.
I'm currently on the lookout for next steps after I leave higher education (expected mid 2026.)

Outside of my education you can find me working on my own personal projects, and when I need to take some time to relax you'll catch me reading science fiction or watching Star Trek.

It is a personal mission of mine to make tech as accessible to as many people as possible. Technology has helped me greatly during my lifetime not only as a deep interest but as a tool used to aid me in day to day life. If in my lifetime I can benefit peoples life thanks to something I helped create then I will feel truly fulfilled.

Thanks for reading!
